STRATEGIC OBJECTIVES BY YEAR

2016


The program is able to manage and coordinate multi-sectoral malaria reduction efforts at all levels

2017


Achieve and sustain protection of at least 85% of the population at risk through recommended malaria prevention measures At least 85% of the population practices correct malaria prevention and management measures All health facilities and District Health Offices report routinely and timely on malaria programme performance; All malaria epidemic prone districts have the capacity for epidemic preparedness and response.

2018


Achieve and sustain at least 90% of malaria cases in the public and private sectors and community level receive prompt treatment according to national guidelines
